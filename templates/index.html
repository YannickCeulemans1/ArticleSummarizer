Welcome to the Natural Language Processing section. It is one of the hottest technology of the moment and is often defined with those words: NLP gives the machines the ability to read, understand and derive meaning from human languages. However, how does it work and how can it be useful?

There are so many ways to express yourself verbally or in written, whether you might use certain words, a different tone, or even sometimes particular expressions. The difficulty in NLP is not to generate data, it is how we can analyse those unstructured data. Thanks to new advanced techniques (such as transformers), it becomes easier and easier to use machines to understand humans by understanding the meaning behind those words (the cognitive way).
